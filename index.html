Research

I'm interested in robot navigation, SLAM, computer vision, optimization, and multi-sensor fusion. Much of my research is enabling robots to perceive the world through fusing multi-modality information from different sensors (visual cameras, IMUs, sonar, wheel encoder, 2D/3D LiDAR, GPS, etc).
